Traditional Shay

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tsp loose black tea
  • 4 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)
  • Cardamom pods (optional)

Brewing Method

  1. Bring water to boil in a teapot
  2. Add tea leaves and optional spices
  3. Simmer for 3-5 minutes
  4. Add sugar and stir
  5. Strain and serve in small glasses

Serving Tradition

Shay is more than a drink - it's a social ritual, often served to guests as a sign of hospitality.
