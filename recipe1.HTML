Traditional Ful Medames

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 2 cups fava beans (soaked overnight)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Method

  1. Drain soaked beans and cook in fresh water until tender (2-3 hours)
  2. Mash beans slightly, leaving some whole
  3. Add garlic, lemon juice, and spices
  4. Simmer for 15 minutes
  5. Drizzle with olive oil before serving

Cultural Significance

Ful Medames is a staple breakfast dish with deep cultural roots, often enjoyed with bread and fresh vegetables.
